What we do
Tend specialises in the management of Australian Government Home Care Packages.

Our all-inclusive service allows you to be in full control of your home care package while also enjoying the expertise and unbiased advice of our Professional Care Managers.  

Your personal Tend Care Managers will;

  • work with you to design a care plan that reflects your needs and goals. 
  • make recommendations from our pool of leading service providers
  • set up your those services to fit your schedule.
  • regularly check in and discuss any adjustments to your care plan
  • evaluate the effectiveness of your services and propose changes if necessary
  • keep a close eye on your home care package funds and your budget
  • ensure that you are getting the best prices for your services.
